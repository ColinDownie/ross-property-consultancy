# Process — Ross Property

## 1. Initial Contact
Client reaches out with a property requirement or project brief.

## 2. Discovery
Clarification of scope, objectives, constraints, and required outputs.

## 3. Proposal
Clear outline of deliverables, timelines, and professional fees.

## 4. Analysis & Assessment
Valuation, feasibility, site review, or due diligence depending on service.

## 5. Documentation
Production of reports, diagrams, layouts, or advisory notes.

## 6. Review & Clarification
Client reviews outputs; amendments made where required.

## 7. Completion
Final documents delivered; project closed or moved to next phase.
