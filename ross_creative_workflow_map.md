# Ross Creative — Multi‑Division Workflow Map

## 1. Intake
Client contacts any division.  
Client Experience AI logs details.  
Project assigned to appropriate division.

---

## 2. Scoping
Division clarifies:
- objectives  
- deliverables  
- timelines  
- required expertise  

Cross‑division support identified if needed.

---

## 3. Proposal
Lead division prepares:
- scope  
- fees  
- timeline  
- deliverables  

---

## 4. Execution
Division executes work with AI support:
- Consulting → strategy  
- Studio → production  
- Property → analysis  
- Design → identity  
- Textiles → materials  
- Digital → automation  

---

## 5. Review
Internal review for:
- clarity  
- accuracy  
- formatting  
- brand alignment  

---

## 6. Delivery
Final outputs delivered to client.  
Clarifications handled.  
Project closed.

---

## 7. Archiving
Documents stored.  
Notes logged.  
Data retained securely.

---

## Cross‑Division Logic
- Shared identity  
- Shared tone  
- Shared structure  
- Division‑specific expertise  
- AI‑supported consistency
