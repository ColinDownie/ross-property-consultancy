The Clarity Layer

Turning complexity into language people can actually use.

Methodology

The Clarity Layer is where we translate structure into understanding. Once the system is mapped, we explain it in plain language for every audience: leadership, teams, clients and technical staff.

This is the Explanation layer of the Six‑Point System: making the complex simple without dumbing it down.

How We Work Here

  • Decode: Read technical, legal and structural detail.
  • Translate: Turn it into clear, human‑readable narratives.
  • Align: Ensure everyone is talking about the same thing.
