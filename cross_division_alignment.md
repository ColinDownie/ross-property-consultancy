# Cross‑Division Identity Alignment  
Ross Creative

## Purpose
Ensures all divisions operate with a unified identity while maintaining functional specialisation.

---

## Shared Elements
- Typography  
- Tone of voice  
- Layout structure  
- Brand philosophy  
- Document architecture  

---

## Division‑Specific Variations
### Ross Consulting
Strategic language, organisational clarity.

### Ross Studio
Production‑focused, artefact‑driven language.

### Ross Property
Spatial, structural, environmental logic.

### Ross Design
Visual identity, brand systems, creative architecture.

### Ross Textiles
Material, pattern, surface language.

### Ross Digital
Automation, AI, digital workflow language.

---

## Alignment Rules
- All divisions use the same structural templates  
- All divisions follow the same tone rules  
- All divisions maintain consistent formatting  
- Each division applies its functional vocabulary  

---

## Outcome
A unified, modern, professional brand system across the entire Ross Creative organisation.
