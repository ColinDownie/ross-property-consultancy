The Outcome Layer

Where decisions become workflows, and workflows become reality.

Workflow Framework

The Outcome Layer is where we turn decisions into concrete workflows, responsibilities and actions. It’s the bridge from “we’ve decided” to “this is how we now work”.

This is the Application layer of the Six‑Point System.

What This Includes

  • Process maps: Who does what, when and with which tools.
  • Ownership: Clear responsibility for each step.
  • Feedback loops: How we learn and improve the workflow over time.
