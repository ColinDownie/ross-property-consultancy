# Client Contract  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## 1. Parties
This agreement is between Ross Property (“Consultant”) and the Client named in the Proposal.

## 2. Scope of Services
Services are defined in the Proposal and may include:
- Valuation  
- Feasibility  
- Site Analysis  
- Due Diligence  
- Advisory  
- Spatial Planning  

## 3. Deliverables
Deliverables are outlined in the Proposal.  
All deliverables are advisory in nature.

## 4. Fees & Payment
Fees are stated in the Proposal.  
Payment terms:
- 50% on instruction  
- 50% on delivery  
Invoices are issued electronically.

## 5. Client Responsibilities
Client must provide accurate information and access to relevant documents.  
Delays in information may affect timelines.

## 6. Limitations
Consultant provides advisory services only.  
Consultant does not provide legal, surveying, engineering, or certified valuation services.

## 7. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of shared information.

## 8. Liability
Consultant is not liable for decisions made by the Client based on advisory outputs.  
Liability is limited to the total fee paid.

## 9. Termination
Either party may terminate with written notice.  
Fees for completed work remain payable.

## 10. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
