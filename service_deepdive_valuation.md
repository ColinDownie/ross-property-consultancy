# Service Deep‑Dive: Property Valuation  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Provides clear, structured valuation logic for residential, commercial, and mixed‑use assets.

## Inputs Required
- Property details  
- Comparable evidence  
- Market data  
- Condition notes  
- Tenure  
- Planning status  

## Methodology
1. Comparable analysis  
2. Market assessment  
3. Adjustment logic  
4. Valuation approach selection  
5. Assumptions and limitations  

## Outputs
- Valuation figure  
- Evidence summary  
- Adjustment notes  
- Market context  
- Assumptions
