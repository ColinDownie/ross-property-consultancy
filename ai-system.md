# AI Assistant System

Ross Property uses a set of integrated AI Assistants to support workflow, documentation, analysis, and client communication.

## Operations AI Lead  
Internal Name: Co3PO  
Coordinates all assistants and manages workflow.

## Financial Intelligence Assistant  
Internal Name: Calc  
Handles valuations, feasibility, modelling, and projections.

## Client Experience Assistant  
Internal Name: Client  
Supports communication, onboarding, and clarity.

## Compliance & Risk Assistant  
Internal Name: Risk  
Manages due diligence, regulatory alignment, and exposure analysis.

## Documentation & Records Assistant  
Internal Name: Scribe  
Produces reports, drafts documents, and maintains records.

## Property & Spatial Intelligence Assistant  
Internal Name: Site  
Provides site analysis, spatial logic, and environmental assessment.
