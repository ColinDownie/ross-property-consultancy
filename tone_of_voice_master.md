# Tone of Voice — Master File  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Core Tone
- Clear  
- Neutral  
- Professional  
- Modern  
- Structured  

## Communication Style
- Short sentences  
- Direct statements  
- Minimal adjectives  
- No emotional language  
- No marketing exaggeration  

## Vocabulary Rules
Preferred:
- “Assessment”  
- “Analysis”  
- “Overview”  
- “Findings”  
- “Recommendation”  

Avoid:
- “Amazing”  
- “Incredible”  
- “Exciting”  
- “Revolutionary”  

## Sentence Structure
- One idea per sentence  
- Logical flow  
- Consistent terminology  
- No filler language  

## Consistency
All client‑facing and internal documents must follow this tone.
