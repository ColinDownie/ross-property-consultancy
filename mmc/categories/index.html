MMC Categories

The seven recognised categories of Modern Methods of Construction.

Categories

  • Category 1 – Pre‑manufactured 3D Volumetric Systems
  • Category 2 – Pre‑manufactured 2D Panelised Systems
  • Category 3 – Pre‑manufactured Components
  • Category 4 – Additive Manufacturing
  • Category 5 – Non‑structural Assemblies
  • Category 6 – Traditional Construction with MMC Enhancements
  • Category 7 – Site Process Optimisation

Usage

Categories help classify MMC solutions and guide procurement and design decisions.
