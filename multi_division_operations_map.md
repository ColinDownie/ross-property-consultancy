# Ross Creative — Multi‑Division Operations Map (Text Logic)

## Overview
This map shows how all divisions interact within a unified operational system.

---

## 1. Intake Layer
- Client contacts any division  
- Client Experience AI logs details  
- Project assigned to lead division  

---

## 2. Scoping Layer
Lead division defines:
- objectives  
- deliverables  
- timelines  
- required expertise  

Cross‑division support identified.

---

## 3. Proposal Layer
Lead division prepares:
- scope  
- fees  
- timeline  
- deliverables  

---

## 4. Execution Layer
### Consulting
Strategy, structure, clarity.

### Studio
Production, artefacts, 3D.

### Property
Valuation, feasibility, due diligence, spatial logic.

### Design
Identity, brand systems, visual architecture.

### Textiles
Materials, surfaces, patterns.

### Digital
Automation, AI, workflow systems.

---

## 5. Review Layer
- Internal review  
- Brand alignment  
- Quality assurance  

---

## 6. Delivery Layer
- Final outputs delivered  
- Clarifications handled  

---

## 7. Archiving Layer
- Documents stored  
- Notes logged  
- Data retained securely
