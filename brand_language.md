# Brand Language — Ross Property

## Core Identity
Ross Property communicates with clarity, precision, and professionalism.  
The brand voice is modern, structured, and confident.

## Tone
- Calm  
- Direct  
- Intelligent  
- Minimalist  
- Advisory  

## Vocabulary
Preferred terms:
- “Assessment”  
- “Analysis”  
- “Overview”  
- “Findings”  
- “Recommendation”  

Avoid:
- Overly technical jargon  
- Emotional language  
- Marketing-style exaggeration  

## Messaging Pillars
1. Clarity  
2. Structure  
3. Professionalism  
4. Modernity  
5. Efficiency  

## Writing Style
- Short sentences  
- Clean structure  
- No unnecessary adjectives  
- Focus on function and logic  

## Consistency
All public‑facing materials must align with these principles.
