# Development Appraisal Model  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Provides a structured narrative for assessing development viability.

---

## 1. Gross Development Value (GDV)
Calculated using:
- comparable sales  
- market evidence  
- projected values  

---

## 2. Development Costs
Includes:
- construction  
- professional fees  
- planning costs  
- finance  
- contingency  

---

## 3. Land Value
Residual calculation:
GDV – Total Costs – Developer Profit = Land Value

---

## 4. Profit Margin
Standard ranges:
- Residential: 15–20%  
- Commercial: 10–15%  
- Mixed‑use: variable  

---

## 5. Sensitivity Testing
Test variations in:
- sales values  
- build costs  
- interest rates  
- timelines  

---

## 6. Conclusion
Advisory appraisal based on available information and market conditions.
