# Typography System — Ross Property

## Primary Typeface
**Inter**  
Usage: All headings, body text, reports, and digital materials

## Secondary Typeface
None  
The brand uses a single, modern, functional typeface for consistency.

---

## Hierarchy
### H1 — Page Titles
- Weight: Bold  
- Size: 28–32px  
- Colour: Slate Black  

### H2 — Section Titles
- Weight: Semi‑Bold  
- Size: 20–24px  
- Colour: Slate Black  

### H3 — Subsections
- Weight: Medium  
- Size: 16–18px  
- Colour: Slate Black  

### Body Text
- Weight: Regular  
- Size: 14–16px  
- Colour: Slate Black  

---

## Rules
- No italics  
- No decorative fonts  
- No script fonts  
- No excessive bolding  
- Maintain consistent spacing  

Typography must remain clean, modern, and functional.
