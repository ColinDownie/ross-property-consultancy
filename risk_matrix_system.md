# Ross Property — Risk Matrix System

## Purpose
Provides a structured method for assessing and categorising risk.

---

## 1. Risk Categories
### High Risk
- Structural issues  
- Title defects  
- Planning breaches  
- Environmental hazards  

### Medium Risk
- Access constraints  
- Service limitations  
- Moderate defects  

### Low Risk
- Minor repairs  
- Cosmetic issues  

---

## 2. Severity Scale
1 — Minimal impact  
2 — Low impact  
3 — Moderate impact  
4 — High impact  
5 — Critical impact  

---

## 3. Likelihood Scale
1 — Rare  
2 — Unlikely  
3 — Possible  
4 — Likely  
5 — Almost certain  

---

## 4. Risk Score
Risk Score = Severity × Likelihood  
Range: 1–25

---

## 5. Mitigation
- Immediate action  
- Monitoring  
- Specialist input  
- Documentation  

---

## 6. Reporting
All risks included in:
- Due diligence reports  
- Feasibility studies  
- Advisory notes
