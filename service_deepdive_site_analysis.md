# Service Deep‑Dive: Site Analysis  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Provides spatial, environmental, and functional assessment of a site.

## Inputs Required
- Site boundaries  
- Access points  
- Surrounding context  
- Environmental factors  
- Planning constraints  

## Methodology
1. Spatial logic review  
2. Environmental assessment  
3. Flow and circulation analysis  
4. Development potential review  

## Outputs
- Spatial assessment  
- Environmental notes  
- Constraints  
- Opportunities
