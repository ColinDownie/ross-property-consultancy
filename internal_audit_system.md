# Ross Property — Internal Audit System

## Purpose
Ensures quality, consistency, and compliance across all projects.

---

## 1. Audit Frequency
- Monthly internal review  
- Quarterly division review  
- Annual cross‑division review  

---

## 2. Audit Areas
### Documentation
- Structure  
- Formatting  
- Tone  
- Accuracy  

### Workflow
- Intake  
- Scoping  
- Proposal  
- Execution  
- Review  
- Delivery  
- Archiving  

### Compliance
- Risk identification  
- Regulatory awareness  
- Data handling  

---

## 3. Audit Process
1. Select sample projects  
2. Review documentation  
3. Assess workflow adherence  
4. Identify issues  
5. Recommend improvements  

---

## 4. Reporting
- Summary of findings  
- Action points  
- Responsible parties  
- Deadlines  

---

## 5. Follow‑Up
- Implementation check  
- Review of corrective actions
