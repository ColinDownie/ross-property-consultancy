What This Page Is

This page defines the Structural Clarity Guide – the first layer of the Ross Six‑Layer Outcome Model. It explains how we bring order to complexity before anything else happens.

Doctrine

Every engagement begins with structure. Before we design, decide or deploy, we map the system: what exists, what connects, what depends on what, and what breaks if the order is wrong.

This is the Sequence layer of the Six‑Point System: the order of operations that prevents downstream chaos.

How This Appears on the Web

Use this page as the “start here” doctrine link in navigation, or as the first step in a “How Ross Works” section. It anchors everything else in structural thinking.
