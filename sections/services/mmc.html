Modern Methods of Construction (MMC)

Precision‑built, sustainable, and delivered with clarity from concept to completion.

Bespoke Modular Building Services

Construction is completed offsite using lean manufacturing techniques to prefabricate single or multi‑storey buildings in deliverable module sections. Modules are typically based on 20‑foot Intermodal container dimensions, adapted with smooth walls, high‑grade finishes, and full MEP integration.

Permanent Modular Construction (PMC) is manufactured in controlled factory environments using wood, steel, or concrete. Modules are 60–90% complete before arriving onsite, reducing disruption and accelerating delivery.

Permanent Modular Construction (PMC)

PMC modules can be standalone or integrated into larger developments. They meet or exceed traditional building standards and can be delivered with fixtures, finishes, and services pre‑installed. Placement onsite typically takes hours to days.
