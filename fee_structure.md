# Fee Structure

### Property Valuation
Fixed fee depending on property type and complexity.

### Feasibility Study
Fixed fee or project‑based fee depending on scope.

Fixed fee based on site size and required depth.

Hourly or fixed fee depending on documentation required.

Hourly or fixed fee depending on complexity.

### Development Advisory
Hourly or retainer‑based.
