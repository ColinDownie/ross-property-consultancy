# Ross Creative — Governance System

## Purpose
Defines how the organisation maintains consistency, quality, and alignment across all divisions.

---

## 1. Brand Governance
- Shared identity  
- Shared tone  
- Shared structure  
- Division‑specific vocabulary  

---

## 2. Documentation Governance
- Standard templates  
- Consistent formatting  
- Professional tone  
- Minimalist design  

---

## 3. Workflow Governance
- Unified workflow stages  
- Division autonomy within shared structure  
- AI‑supported consistency  

---

## 4. Quality Assurance
- Internal review  
- Cross‑division checks where required  
- Brand alignment verification  

---

## 5. Data Governance
- Confidentiality  
- Secure storage  
- Minimal retention  
- Controlled access  

---

## 6. AI Governance
- AI supports, does not replace  
- All outputs reviewed  
- No legal or certified claims
