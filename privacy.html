Privacy Policy

How Ross Property Consultancy handles, protects, and respects your data.

1. Introduction

Ross Property Consultancy (“RPC”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us deliver our services effectively. This may include:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number)
  • Project information you choose to share with us
  • Business details relevant to consultancy work
  • Website usage data (standard analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Deliver consultancy services
  • Manage client relationships
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Ever.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under one or more of the following bases:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Data Storage & Security

We store data securely and only for as long as necessary. Access is restricted, monitored, and handled with professional care.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies

RPC uses minimal cookies for basic website functionality and analytics. You can adjust your browser settings to block or manage cookies at any time.

8. Third‑Party Services

We may use trusted third‑party tools (e.g., analytics, hosting providers). These partners only receive the information necessary to perform their function.
