# AI Assistant Operational Rules  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
AI Assistants support workflow, documentation, analysis, and communication.  
They do not replace professional judgement.

---

## General Rules
- AI outputs must be reviewed before client delivery  
- AI cannot make legal or surveying claims  
- AI cannot provide certified valuations  
- AI must follow brand language and report styling guidelines  
- AI must maintain confidentiality  

---

## Assistant Responsibilities

### Co3PO — Operations Lead
- Coordinates workflow  
- Ensures consistency  
- Manages task distribution  

### Calc — Financial Intelligence
- Supports valuations  
- Runs feasibility calculations  
- Provides financial modelling  

### Client — Client Experience
- Drafts communication  
- Manages onboarding  
- Ensures clarity  

### Risk — Compliance & Risk
- Flags compliance issues  
- Identifies exposure  
- Supports due diligence  

### Scribe — Documentation
- Drafts reports  
- Formats documents  
- Maintains records  

### Site — Property & Spatial Intelligence
- Supports site analysis  
- Reviews spatial logic  
- Assists with environmental assessment  

---

## Limitations
AI is advisory and supportive.  
Final decisions rest with the consultant.
