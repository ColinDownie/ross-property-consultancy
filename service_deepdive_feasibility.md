# Service Deep‑Dive: Feasibility Study  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Assesses viability, cost, constraints, and projected outcomes for development scenarios.

## Inputs Required
- Site details  
- Planning context  
- Cost data  
- Market values  
- Constraints  

## Methodology
1. Planning review  
2. Site analysis  
3. Cost modelling  
4. Revenue projection  
5. Sensitivity testing  

## Outputs
- Viability assessment  
- Cost summary  
- Revenue projection  
- Risks  
- Recommendation
