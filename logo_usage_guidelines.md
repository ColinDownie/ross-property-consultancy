# Logo Usage Guidelines  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Logo Structure
The Ross Property logo is a simple typographic mark:
“Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative”

## Clear Space
Maintain a clear space equal to the height of the capital “R” around all sides.

## Minimum Size
Do not display the logo smaller than 120px width in digital formats.

## Colour Usage
- Primary: Slate Black (#1A1A1A)  
- Reverse: Structural White (#FFFFFF)  

No other colours permitted.

## Backgrounds
- Use on white or light grey backgrounds  
- Do not place on photography  
- Do not place on patterned surfaces  

## Prohibited Uses
- No stretching  
- No rotating  
- No shadows  
- No gradients  
- No colour changes  
- No additional symbols  

The logo must remain clean, modern, and consistent.
