# Ross Property — Pricing Architecture

## Pricing Model
Combination of fixed fees, hourly rates, and project‑based pricing.

## Tier 1 — Core Advisory
Fixed fee:
- Valuation  
- Site analysis  
- Due diligence  
- Advisory notes  

## Tier 2 — Development Services
Fixed or project‑based:
- Feasibility  
- Development appraisal  
- Spatial planning  

## Tier 3 — Strategic Support
Hourly or retainer:
- Acquisition support  
- Disposal strategy  
- Development strategy  

## Pricing Principles
- Transparent  
- Structured  
- Consistent  
- Aligned with deliverables
