# Ross Property — Client Journey Map

## Stage 1 — Awareness
Client identifies a property need:
- valuation  
- feasibility  
- due diligence  
- site analysis  
- advisory  

## Stage 2 — Initial Contact
Client reaches out via email.  
Client Experience Assistant logs details.

## Stage 3 — Discovery
Consultant clarifies:
- objectives  
- constraints  
- timelines  
- required outputs  

## Stage 4 — Proposal
Proposal issued with:
- scope  
- deliverables  
- fees  
- timeline  

## Stage 5 — Instruction
Client accepts proposal.  
Project is opened.

## Stage 6 — Analysis
Depending on service:
- valuation  
- feasibility  
- site review  
- due diligence  
- advisory  

AI Assistants support calculations, documentation, and compliance.

## Stage 7 — Documentation
Draft report produced.  
Internal review.  
Amendments made.

## Stage 8 — Delivery
Final report delivered.  
Clarifications handled.

## Stage 9 — Completion
Project closed.  
Documents archived.  
Notes logged.
