# Service Deep‑Dive: Due Diligence  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Identifies risks, compliance gaps, and structural or legal issues.

## Inputs Required
- Title documents  
- Planning history  
- Condition information  
- Compliance records  

## Methodology
1. Title review  
2. Planning review  
3. Condition review  
4. Compliance review  
5. Risk scoring  

## Outputs
- Risk register  
- Compliance notes  
- Condition summary  
- Recommendations
