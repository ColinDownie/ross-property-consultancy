Client‑Side PMO Lite

Lightweight project management support without unnecessary bureaucracy.

What PMO Lite Is

PMO Lite is a streamlined project support model designed to keep developments organised, aligned, and moving — without the overhead of a full project management office.

Core Functions

  • Tracking and reporting on progress
  • Coordinating between teams and disciplines
  • Risk logging and issue management
  • Light governance and decision support
  • Maintaining clarity on actions and responsibilities

When It’s Useful

  • Early‑stage projects
  • Smaller teams or lean organisations
  • Clients without internal PMO capability

Benefits

  • Clear structure and visibility
  • Reduced friction between parties
  • Better communication and coordination
  • Stronger, more timely decision‑making
