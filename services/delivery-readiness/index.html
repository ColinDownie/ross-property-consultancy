Delivery Readiness

Preparing projects for the move from design into delivery with clarity and control.

What Delivery Readiness Means

Delivery Readiness is the structured bridge between concept and execution. It ensures that design, programme, procurement, and risk are aligned before a project moves into delivery.

Key Components

  • Programme clarity and critical path awareness
  • Procurement strategy and route selection
  • Risk mapping and mitigation planning
  • Governance setup and decision structures
  • Stakeholder roles and responsibilities

Why It Matters

A clear, structured approach at this stage reduces uncertainty, strengthens decision‑making, and helps projects enter delivery with confidence and control.
