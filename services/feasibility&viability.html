What This Service Does

Feasibility & Viability provides structured, early‑stage analysis to determine whether a development opportunity is financially, technically, and operationally deliverable. We assess cost, risk, density, planning context, and commercial performance to help you make informed decisions before committing capital.

Core Components

  • Financial Modelling — Appraisals, cost plans, yield analysis, and sensitivity testing.
  • Development Capacity — Density, massing, layout potential, and site optimisation.
  • Constraint Review — Technical, environmental, legal, and planning constraints that impact viability.
  • Scenario Testing — Multiple development options assessed for cost, risk, and commercial performance.
  • Go/No‑Go Recommendations — Clear, structured advice on whether to progress, pause, or pivot.

Why It Matters

Early‑stage feasibility prevents costly design iterations, reduces commercial risk, and ensures development decisions are grounded in evidence. This service gives clients a clear, commercially aligned foundation before moving into design or acquisition.

Who This Is For

Developers, investors, landowners, and joint venture partners who require structured, commercially robust feasibility analysis to support confident decision‑making.

How We Work

We combine financial modelling with planning intelligence and operational insight. Our approach is disciplined, transparent, and aligned with real‑world delivery, ensuring every recommendation is practical and commercially sound.
