Commercial Assurance

Independent review of cost, value, procurement, and risk.

What We Review

Commercial Assurance provides independent testing of the commercial assumptions behind a project. We review cost plans, procurement strategies, value exposure, and risk.

  • Cost plans and budgets
  • Procurement routes and strategies
  • Value and revenue assumptions
  • Risk exposure and contingency

How We Provide Assurance

We test assumptions, validate information, and provide clear, structured recommendations. Our focus is on transparency, commercial integrity, and alignment with client objectives.

Typical Issues Identified

  • Overstated value or optimistic assumptions
  • Underestimated risk or missing contingencies
  • Misaligned procurement strategies
  • Gaps in commercial controls or reporting

Outcomes

  • Greater transparency
  • Improved confidence in decisions
  • Stronger commercial discipline
