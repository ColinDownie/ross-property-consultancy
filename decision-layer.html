The Decision Layer

Where options become choices – on purpose, not by accident.

Diagnostic Role

The Decision Layer is where we surface options, trade‑offs and consequences so leaders can make informed choices. It’s the point where structure, clarity, interpretation and detail all converge.

This is the Selection layer of the Six‑Point System.

Diagnostic Questions

  • What are the viable options?
  • What does each option cost – in money, time and risk?
  • What happens if we do nothing?
  • What breaks if we choose wrong?

Web Inclusion

Use this page as a diagnostic tool: a place where decision‑makers can see how Ross frames choices and trade‑offs.
