# Valuation Methodology  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
This document outlines the internal logic used to produce advisory valuations.

---

## 1. Information Collection
- Property address  
- Tenure  
- Size  
- Condition  
- Use class  
- Planning status  
- Comparable evidence  
- Market data  

---

## 2. Market Analysis
- Local supply and demand  
- Recent sales  
- Rental evidence  
- Yield trends  
- Economic context  

---

## 3. Comparable Assessment
Comparables are assessed based on:
- proximity  
- property type  
- size  
- condition  
- date of sale  
- adjustments required  

---

## 4. Valuation Approaches

### Market Approach
Based on comparable sales adjusted for:
- size  
- condition  
- location  
- features  

### Income Approach
Used for investment property:
- rental income  
- operating costs  
- yield  
- capitalisation  

### Residual Approach
Used for development:
- GDV  
- build costs  
- fees  
- finance  
- profit margin  

---

## 5. Assumptions & Limitations
- Based on available information  
- Advisory only  
- Not a certified valuation  
- Specialist input may be required  

---

## 6. Conclusion
Valuation is expressed as a reasoned estimate supported by evidence and analysis.
