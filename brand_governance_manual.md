# Ross Creative — Brand Governance Manual (Expanded)

## Purpose
Ensures consistent application of the Ross Creative identity across all divisions.

---

## 1. Identity Rules
- Inter typeface only  
- Neutral colour palette  
- Minimalist layout  
- No decorative elements  

---

## 2. Tone of Voice
- Clear  
- Neutral  
- Professional  
- Structured  
- Modern  

---

## 3. Documentation Standards
- Defined hierarchy  
- Consistent spacing  
- Clean structure  
- Professional formatting  

---

## 4. Division Alignment
All divisions follow:
- shared templates  
- shared tone  
- shared structure  
- division‑specific vocabulary  

---

## 5. Governance Process
- Quarterly review  
- Cross‑division audit  
- Update of templates  
- Enforcement of standards
