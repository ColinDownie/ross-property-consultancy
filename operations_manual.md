# Internal Operations Manual  
Ross Property — A Division of Ross Creative

## Purpose
Defines the internal structure, workflow, and operational standards.

---

## 1. Roles & Responsibilities
### Principal Consultant
- Oversees all projects  
- Reviews all outputs  
- Makes final decisions  

### AI Assistants
- Support analysis  
- Draft documentation  
- Maintain workflow  
- Ensure consistency  

---

## 2. Workflow Stages
1. Intake  
2. Scoping  
3. Proposal  
4. Instruction  
5. Analysis  
6. Documentation  
7. Delivery  
8. Archiving  

---

## 3. Quality Standards
- Clear structure  
- Consistent formatting  
- Accurate data  
- Reviewed before delivery  
- Aligned with brand language  

---

## 4. Communication Standards
- Professional tone  
- Clear expectations  
- Defined timelines  
- Transparent updates  

---

## 5. Data Handling
- Confidential  
- Secure storage  
- Minimal retention  
- Used only for project delivery  

---

## 6. Review Process
- Draft created  
- Internal review  
- Amendments  
- Final approval
